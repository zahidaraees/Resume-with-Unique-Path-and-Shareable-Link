What Data We Store

  • All resume content you enter (personal info, skills, education, experience)
  • Your selected template, font, and color preferences
  • Usage statistics (stored locally only)

What We DON'T Store

  • No server-side storage of any kind
  • No tracking cookies or analytics sent to third parties
  • No profile photos stored on servers (stored locally only)

Your Rights
